About Me

I’m a Dublin-based senior graphic designer and motion graphics artist with over eight years of experience creating thoughtful visual work that communicates clearly and connects with audiences. My background spans brand identity, digital campaigns, print design, motion graphics, and visual storytelling. Whether I’m building brand systems, shaping campaign assets, or designing interactive experiences, I bring a balance of creativity, clarity, and practical problem solving to every project. At the core of my approach is a focus on visual storytelling. I care deeply about typography, layout, motion, and hierarchy, and how these elements work together to guide people through information in a way that feels natural and engaging across both digital and print. My goal is always to create design that not only looks strong, but adds real value to how a message is understood.
